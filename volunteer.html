Why Volunteer With Us?

Volunteering at Paws & Hearts is a rewarding experience that allows you to make a real difference in the lives of animals. Whether you have a few hours a week or can only help on weekends, every moment you give helps us provide better care for our furry friends.

Volunteer Opportunities

🐕

Dog Walker

Take our dogs on walks and help them get exercise and socialization. Perfect for active individuals who love the outdoors.

  • Time: 2-4 hours/week
  • Training: Required
  • Experience: None needed
🐈

Cat Socializer

Spend time playing with and socializing our cats to help them become more comfortable with people.

  • Time: 2-3 hours/week
  • Training: Basic orientation
  • Experience: None needed
🏠

Foster Parent

Provide temporary homes for animals in need. Great for those who can't commit to permanent adoption.

  • Time: Flexible duration
  • Training: Comprehensive
  • Experience: Preferred
🎉

Event Helper

Assist at adoption events, fundraisers, and community outreach programs throughout the year.

  • Time: Events vary
  • Training: On-site briefing
  • Experience: None needed
📋

Administrative Support

Help with office tasks, data entry, answering phones, and general administrative duties.

  • Time: 3-5 hours/week
  • Training: Basic orientation
  • Experience: Computer skills
📸

Photographer

Take photos of our animals for social media and adoption profiles to help them find homes faster.

  • Time: Flexible schedule
  • Training: Not required
  • Experience: Photography skills

Volunteer Benefits

Make a Real Impact

Your time directly improves the lives of animals in our care and helps them find loving homes.

Learn New Skills

Gain experience in animal care, customer service, and develop valuable professional skills.

Meet Like-Minded People

Join a community of passionate animal lovers and make lasting friendships.

Flexible Schedule

We work with your availability to find volunteer shifts that fit your lifestyle.

Volunteer Recognition

Receive certificates, appreciation events, and references for your dedication.

Personal Fulfillment

Experience the joy and satisfaction that comes from helping animals in need.

Volunteer Requirements

General Requirements

  • Must be at least 16 years old (some roles require 18+)
  • Commit to a minimum of 3 months of volunteering
  • Attend mandatory orientation session
  • Complete required training for your chosen role
  • Pass a background check (for certain positions)
  • Provide proof of tetanus vaccination

What to Expect

  • Orientation session (2 hours)
  • Role-specific training (varies by position)
  • Shadowing experienced volunteers
  • Ongoing support from staff members
  • Monthly volunteer meetings
  • Opportunities for advancement

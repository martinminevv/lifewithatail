Common Questions

Do I need an appointment to visit?

Walk-ins are welcome during our regular hours. However, we recommend scheduling an appointment for adoption consultations to ensure we can give you our full attention.

Can I bring my current pet to meet a potential new companion?

Yes! We encourage meet-and-greets with your current pets. Please call ahead to arrange a suitable time for introductions.

How quickly will I receive a response?

We typically respond to emails and phone messages within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call us during our operating hours.

Do you accept donations at the shelter?

Absolutely! We accept monetary donations, pet supplies, and your time through volunteering. Visit us during open hours or check our website for our donation wishlist.

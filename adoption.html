Adoption Requirements

Age Requirement

Adopters must be at least 21 years old and provide valid identification.

Home Visit

We conduct a home visit to ensure a safe and suitable environment for your new pet.

Landlord Approval

Renters must provide written permission from their landlord allowing pets on the property.

Family Agreement

All household members must meet the pet and agree to the adoption before finalizing.

Adoption Fee

Fees range from $50-$200 and cover vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and microchipping.

Time Commitment

Be prepared to provide proper care, attention, and veterinary treatment for the life of your pet.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the adoption process take?

The entire process typically takes 3-7 days from application to bringing your pet home. This includes application review, meet-and-greet, and home visit.

What is included in the adoption fee?

All pets are spayed/neutered, vaccinated, microchipped, and have received a health check. The fee also includes a starter kit with food, toys, and care information.

Can I adopt if I have other pets?

Yes! We encourage meet-and-greets with your current pets to ensure compatibility. Proof of current pet vaccinations is required.

What if the adoption doesn't work out?

We offer a 30-day trial period. If there are issues, we'll work with you to resolve them or help facilitate a return if absolutely necessary.
